Disqus Comments

| Comments

Octopress generates static sites. For your site to support comments you will need to use a third party service to host your comments.

Octopress has plugin support to Disqus and it’s pretty easy to setup.

1. Signup to Disqus

Head to disqus and sign yourself up. Keep note of the site short name they provide.

2. Edit _config.yml

That should be pretty much it.
